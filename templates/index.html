Genres I enjoy

  • Fighting Games

    • I have always been a fan of fighting games because they have a large replay value due to them having large rosters (which can be expanded upon). They work well as both a casual and competitive game as the controls can be mastered through combos or players can simply noodle around on their controllers or button mash. More complexity can be found through learning in game frame data, character hitboxes, etc. I have always wanted to make my own fighting game because I am fascinated by the mechanics and would like to make my own unique fighting game to add to the genre.
