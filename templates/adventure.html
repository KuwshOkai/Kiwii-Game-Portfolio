Adventure Game Ideas

    "Paradise Lost"

This adventure game idea is one i've had for a while now. Essentially, the player character is cast out of "heaven" and is forced to wander through "hell".

In the start of the game, the character does not remember why they were cast out of heaven and as such wanders around aimlessly, or so it seems.

In truth the player character was tasked with cleansing sin from the world and only when they accomplish this can they return to heaven.

To cleanse this "sin" the player encounters multiple phenomena on their journey, be it a labyrinth or a monster terrorising a town, and each of these phenomena

are actually a physical representation of a sin (which in this case will be based off of the seven deadly sins).

As the player purges the world of these sins they find items that are akin to pieces of their own soul and with each of these items the character gets a memory of why

they were kicked out of heaven.
